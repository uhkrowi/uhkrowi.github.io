Meeting Room Management

ClientPT B-Solution (part of Indovisual Investama)Project TypeFull-stack Web and Mobile DevelopmentYear2020

A mobile application serves as a comprehensive meeting management tool, offering features such as scheduling meetings, booking available meeting rooms, taking notes during meetings (MOM), inviting participants, and even extending meeting durations when necessary. Additionally, our app allows users to conveniently order meals to the pantry, ensuring a seamless and productive meeting experience.
