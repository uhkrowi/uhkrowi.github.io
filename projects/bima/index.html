BIMA: Lecturers Research Management System

ClientPT Idea Nusantara CiptaProject TypeBack-end Web developmentYear2023

Indonesian Lecturers' Research Management System is designed to streamline and enhance the research process for lecturers. This comprehensive system not only simplifies the proposal submission process but also streamlines evaluations and other essential research-related procedures. Our goal is to provide lecturers with a user-friendly platform that optimizes every aspect of their research endeavors.
