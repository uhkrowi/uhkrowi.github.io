Ship Rental Management
Client-Project TypePrototypeYear2020

This Ship Rental Management system is designed to simplify the process of renting ships for fishermen. It offers a user-friendly platform for selecting the desired type of ship, setting rental periods, extending leases, and tracking payments. Additionally, the application provides valuable tools for ship rental owners, allowing them to efficiently manage ship information, customer data, payments, and generate reports.
