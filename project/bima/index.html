BIMA (Basis Informasi Penelitian dan Pengabdian kepada Masyarakat) is a centralized platform developed for the Ministry of Higher Education, Science, and Technology (Kemdiktisaintek). Ideally designed to support higher education institutions, it facilitates the entire lifecycle of research and community service activities. The platform allows lecturers to submit proposals, lets reviewers evaluate them, and enables the monitoring of funded activities through systematic reporting.

üéØ Features

  • Proposal Submission: A streamlined process for lecturers to create and submit research and community service proposals.
  • Review Process: Tools for assigned reviewers to assess proposals efficiently.
  • Progress Reporting: Features for tracking the implementation and outcomes of funded projects.
  • Data Integration: Seamlessly connects with other education systems like PDDIKTI and SINTA to ensure data consistency.

Built using Go and PostgreSQL, the system is designed to handle high concurrency and ensure data integrity for critical academic records.
