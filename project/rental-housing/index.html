Rental Housing Indonesia is a centralized platform designed to bridge the gap between housing supply and demand across the country. Its primary mission is to provide accessible and affordable housing solutions, catering specifically to low-income citizens as well as the general public looking for rental properties. The platform facilitates a seamless user experience, enabling citizens to browse, book, and secure housing that fits their specific needs and budget.

üéØ Key Features

  • Regional-Based Search: To ensure relevant matches, users are required to register and search within their specific regions. This localization helps in effectively managing demand and supply in specific areas.
  • Diverse Property Types: The platform aggregates various types of housing listings, including:
    • Kontrakan & Kos: Private rental houses and boarding houses for individuals and families.
    • Rusunawa: Low-cost public rental apartments managed by the government for eligible citizens.
  • User-Centric Design: Features a clean interface with dedicated sections for News (Berita), Help (Bantuan), and Contact (Kontak) to assist users throughout their housing search journey.
  • Registration & Verification: A secure login and registration system ensures that applicants are verified and matched with appropriate housing options available in their domicile.
